How to Use Blockchain Voting

Follow these simple steps to participate securely in the blockchain-based election

Install & Setup MetaMask

1
Download MetaMask
Install MetaMask from the official website or browser extension store.
Download MetaMask
2
Create or Import Wallet
Set up a new wallet or import an existing one using your secret recovery phrase.
3
Switch to Correct Network
Ensure you are on the correct blockchain network for the election.

Connect to Voting Platform

1
Open Voting Portal
Visit the official blockchain voting website.
2
Connect MetaMask
Click "Connect Wallet" and approve the connection in MetaMask.
3
Verify Address
Ensure your Ethereum address is displayed correctly before voting.

Cast Your Vote

1
Select a Candidate
Choose your preferred candidate from the list.
2
Confirm Your Choice
Double-check your selection before submitting.
3
Submit Vote
Click "Submit Vote" and confirm the transaction in MetaMask.
